    <texte var="LIB_RUBRIQUE_RESUME" stl="title">
Summary
    </texte>
    <texte var="RUBRIQUE_RESUME" stl="text">
This course provides the foundations of sustainable management and finance : reviewed concepts include market failures and externalities, CSR, green finance, ESG, climate risk pricing. Includes case studies, policy tools, and corporate strategies for sustainability and climate adaptation.
    </texte>
    <texte var="LIB_RUBRIQUE_CONTENU" stl="title">
Content
    </texte>
    <texte var="RUBRIQUE_CONTENU" stl="text">
<p><br />Session 1 Foundations of the Mixed Economic System<p>&#160;</p>* The Invisible Hand and the Welfare Theorems<br />* Liberalism and Competition<br />* Planetary Boundaries: A Major Economic Challenge<br />* Missing Markets and Externalities<br />* The Tragedy of the Commons<br />* GDP vs. GrDP<br />* Alternative Public Levers to Improve System Performance and Associated Challenges<p>&#160;</p>Session 2  The Firm and Sustainable Management<p>&#160;</p>* Shareholder Value Maximization vs. Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)<br />* The Benefit Corporation Movement<br />* Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI)<br />* Public-Private Partnerships (PPP) in Regulation<br />* Environmental Backlash<br />* Key Reports and Perspectives:<br /> * The Draghi Report<br /> * IMD Competitiveness Report<p>&#160;</p><br />Session 3 Sustainable Finance &amp; Asset Pricing<p>&#160;</p>* Introduction to Asset Pricing<br />* Markowitz Portfolio Theory and the CAPM<br />* Integrating Climate Risks into Asset Pricing Models<br />* Market Equilibrium and the Cost of Capital Channel<br />* Divestment Strategies and Sustainability Impacts<p>&#160;</p>Session 4 Sustainable Finance &amp; Corporate Finance<p>&#160;</p>* Introduction to Capital Structure: Debt vs. Equity<br />* Sustainable Equity Investment:<br /> * Shareholder Activism<br /> * CEO Compensation and ESG Alignment<br />* ESG Integration in Corporate Strategy<br />* Sustainable Debt Instruments:<br /> * Green Bonds<br /> * Sustainability-Linked Bonds<br /> * Innovative Financing Frameworks<br />* Greenwashing: Challenges and Detection<p>&#160;</p>Session 5 Management: Selected Sustainability Topics<p>&#160;</p>* Growth vs. De-growth<br />* Resource Scarcity and Strategic Responses<br />* Rising Inequality<br />* The Circular Economy Objective<br />* Managing Natural Capital<br />* Case Studies: Corporate Sustainability Strategies (e.g., Holcim, NestlÃ©, LO)<br />* The Emission Trading System<br />* Externalities in Innovation and Industrial Policy<br />* Poster Topics and Group Discussions<p>&#160;</p>Session 6 Sustainable Finance: Advanced Topics &amp; Poster Projects<p>&#160;</p>* Carbon Offset Technologies:<br /> * CCUS vs. Nature-Based Solutions (Benefits &amp; Risks)<br />* Financing Green Innovation<br />* Climate Adaptation vs. Mitigation<br />* The Role of Insurance Markets in Sustainability<p>&#160;</p><br /></p>
